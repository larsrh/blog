Me no Spam

Stefan Tilkov, Sep 13, 2003

While the volume of SobigF-related emails has gone down somewhat, I now receive about 10-50 emails per day from helpful Mailer-Daemons that want to tell me that the spam I sent to one of their users could not be delivered. So obviously someone is using my mail address - most likely along with millions of others that his software chooses from randomly - as the From: value. This doesn't even serve a purpose for the spammer. So why are they doing it? They really must love being hated.
