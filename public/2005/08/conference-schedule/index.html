Conference Schedule

Stefan Tilkov, Aug 25, 2005

I’ll be speaking at, and visit, several conferences within the next few months: First, I’ll be talking about “SOA and Web Services” (what a great title :-) ) at SOA Days 2005, a conference organized by Deutsche Post AG in Bonn, Germany, on Septemper 14/15. At September 27, I’ll give a talk about “Multi-Paradigm Modeling” at JAOO Denmark — this is one conference I’m particularly looking forward to because I have only heard good things about it. On November 17, my talk at the excellently organized, independent OpenArchitecture conference in Bonn is about Web services standards. On November 23, I will talk about “SOA and MDA” at SOA-Forum 2005 in Bad Homburg, Germany.
