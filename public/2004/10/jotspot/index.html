JotSpot

Stefan Tilkov, Oct 12, 2004

The folks from JotSpot demoed their application to Jon Udell in a WebEx session, and he has put the demo online. This is really cool - not only do I like the software idea (sort of like a crossover of Wiki and Lotus Notes with some RSS and Web services thrown in for good measure), but also the presentation: this is is a great way to find out what a piece of software does — someone demoing it, with someone else asking questions that very often resemble what I would have asked myself.
