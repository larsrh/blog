ZapThink Acquires Linthicum Group

Stefan Tilkov, Sep 5, 2007

ZapThink, LLC, the industry’s foremost advisory firm focused on Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) today announces the acquisition of The Linthicum Group, LLC, a well-respected SOA consulting firm, and the addition of David S. Linthicum to ZapThink.

Very nice. ZapThink is probably the SOA company with the highest per-employee revenue :-)
