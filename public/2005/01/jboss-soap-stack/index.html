JBoss SOAP Stack

Stefan Tilkov, Jan 26, 2005

Steve comments on the JBoss SOAP Stack. I share his view (or at least what I perceive to be his view :-): It’s hard enough to maintain one Open Source stack, and the last thing anybody benefits from is having a fork — especially not one that is driven by Sun’s API-centric view of Web Services as evidenced in the JCP specs.
