REST at JBoss User Group Munich

Stefan Tilkov, Jun 7, 2009

Tomorrow evening, I'll give a talk on REST and JSR 311 (JAX-RS) at the Munich JBoss User Group - this time together with my colleague Serge Pagop, who will add RESTeasy-specific aspects. Very much looking forward to it – I've come to enjoy these informal talks at least as much as those at larger conferences.
