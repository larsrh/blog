Prayer Teams

Stefan Tilkov, Jun 13, 2003

You have to love this:

If you have placed faith in Jesus Christ and desire to help others walk free from sex addiction, please consider joining our prayer teams.

And that you would ask our Lord Jesus to reconcile through his life giving blood, the Reconciliation of my wife to himself (Jesus) and our Marriage, Please help me wage war and save my family, and allow my Children to see the power of a praying body, that they may serve the Lord all there lives, I am indebted to you may God bless you your church and family.
