Word Value

Stefan Tilkov, Nov 16, 2005

Tim Bray:

So, I understand why we still need spreadsheets and presentation packages, but assuming you had a Web editor with a good change tracker, why would anyone want a word processor any more?

I couldn’t agree more: The change tracking feature is the single best reason to stick with Word for me. And that’s how many percent of Word’s feature list — 2? 3?
