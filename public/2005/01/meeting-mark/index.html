Meeting Mark

Stefan Tilkov, Jan 10, 2005

Since he is staying in Düsseldorf for a W3C meeting, Mark Baker got in touch and we scheduled our own face-to-face meeting (a dinner with Deer Ragout, Pork Knuckle and German beer in one of the Old Towns breweries). I really enjoyed it — it’s very cool to finally meet someone in person you have interacted with only virtually before. We actually managed to keep tech-talk to a minimum, but needless to say, I have some REST-related re-thinking of my own ideas to do now :-)
