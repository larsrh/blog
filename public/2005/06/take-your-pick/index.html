Take your Pick

Stefan Tilkov, Jun 14, 2005

I need some pictures of myself suitable for publication in conference material, magazine articles etc. One of the things I throughly hate is having my own picture taken — or, to be more exact, looking at the results, similar to the effect that listening to their own recorded voice has on others. I was able to resist requests to update an old picture that I used send out without much thought in the past, but recently folks have started to threaten to no longer co-author anything with me if I don’t update it :-)

So I’d like to ask for your help in picking the least gruesome looking of these four photographs — click to enlarge them to their full, terror-inspiring size …
