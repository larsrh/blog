RESTful robotics @ MS

Stefan Tilkov, Sep 16, 2007

Paul Sandoz:

I am rather impressed by the application of the REST architectural style to the Robotics Studio architecture, and especially impressive is the light weight service model that enables loosely coupled composition and orchestration of services.
