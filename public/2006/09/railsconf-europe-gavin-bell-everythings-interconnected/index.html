RailsConf Europe: Gavin Bell: Everything's Interconnected

Stefan Tilkov, Sep 14, 2006

Gavin Bell and Tom Armitage gave a talk on the patterns of interconnection they found while creating the Nature Network Boston. This was really more of keynote-style thing, very interesting but hard to summarize while doing it justice. The two presenters had great slides and delivered it perfectly — very much recommended in case you get a chance to see them somewhere.
