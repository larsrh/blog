WS-Transfer

Stefan Tilkov, Mar 26, 2006

I’m late in linking to all of this, but still: Mark Baker calls bullshit on WS-Transfer, and quite a few people join him. Although I have the utmost respect for many of the spec’s authors, I can’t help but agree — I didn’t like WS-Transfer one and a half years ago, and I still don’t see why I would not stick with plain HTTP in situations where I want to use a uniform API. And with regards to any of the experts, or even spec authors, actually rushing to defend their work, the silence is deafening.
