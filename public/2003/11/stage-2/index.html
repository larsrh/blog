Stage 2

Stefan Tilkov, Nov 3, 2003



It’s going to take some time, but at least there is progress … I made a mistake when I ordered the system: I specified ELV (the so-called “Lastschriftverfahren”) as the method of payment; this means that Apple directly transfers money from my bank account to theirs. What I didn’t know is that they then wait for 5 days (!) to make sure I don’t cancel the transfer — so if you want to get something fast from them (and I very much do), you’re better of using a credit card for your purchase. Unfortunately, the only way to change the method of payment was to place a second order and cancel the first one. Anyway, I hope it’s worth the wait ;-)
