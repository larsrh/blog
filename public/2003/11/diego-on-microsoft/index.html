Diego on Microsoft

Stefan Tilkov, Nov 22, 2003

Diego Doval takes a a walk down memory lane and looks at Microsoft’s history of past announcements and real products. Though probably similar stories could be told about almost any other vendor as well, it’s still very interesting.

Whatever Longhorn will look like when it finally ships, it very likely will be a lot different than what we’re led to expect now.
