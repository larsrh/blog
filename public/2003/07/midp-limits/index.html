MIDP limits

Stefan Tilkov, Jul 16, 2003

Diego writes about the limits of MIDP. What annoys me most (not that I care that much) is that there is no way to access the main phone function - placing a telephone call. I believe an extremely useful application of MIDP could then be an mobile phone-integrating CRM or sales support system.
