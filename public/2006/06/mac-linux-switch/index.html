Mac->Linux Switch

Stefan Tilkov, Jun 16, 2006

Mark Pilgrim switches from Mac to Linux, John Gruber has a great response, Tim Bray announces his intention to switch, too.

I’m way too happy to have something that actually works great out of the box, and runs all the open source and commercial software I care about, to even consider this. But I absolutely understand and respect Mark and Tim’s reasoning.
